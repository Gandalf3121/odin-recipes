Buns

Ingredients

  • 400 g wheat flour
  • 200 g warm water
  • 7g dry yeast
  • 3 tbsp olive or oil
  • 1 tbs of salt and sugar
  • 1 small egg and some poppy seeds

Preparation

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add the wet ingredients and mix.
  3. Knead the dought thoroughly and form it into a ball.
  4. Put the dough in a covered bowl and move to a warm place to let it grow.
  5. Split the dought into 8 equal parts and form each into a ball.
  6. Brush the buns with some whipped egg and sprinkle it with some poppy seeds.
  7. Bake for around 20 minutes in 190°C.

And you are finished. Enjoy!
