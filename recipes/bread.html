Bread

Ingredients

  • 500 g wheat flour
  • 7 g dry yeast
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp oats
  • 400 ml warm water
  • (optional) spices; 2 tbs oregano
  • a bit of oil and breadcrumbs to grease the form

Preparation

  1. Add the yeast to warm water, mix nad put aside for a few minutes.
  2. Mix other dry ingredients in a big bowl and pour the yeast with water to it.
  3. Mix everything thoroughly until a compact mass is obtained, then leave the dough in a covered bowl for 30 minutes to let it rise.
  4. Knead the risen dough gently and put it into a greased and lightly dusted baking tin
  5. Put the dough in a warm place to grow for a few minutes and put it into a preheated to 200°C oven.
  6. After around an hour remove from the oven and wait around 15 minutes to let it cool down.

And you are finished. Enjoy!
